RAISE Program Overview

Driving innovation through interdisciplinary research for sustainable development

Rationale & Vision

The RAISE (Research and Innovation for Sustainable Excellence) Program was established in 2018 as a strategic initiative to foster interdisciplinary research and innovation across multiple domains. Recognizing the complex challenges facing our global community, we bring together experts from diverse fields to collaborate on solutions that drive sustainable development.

Since its inception, the RAISE Program has secured over $25 million in research funding, published more than 150 peer-reviewed papers, and established partnerships with 30+ institutions worldwide. Our vision is to become a global leader in transformative research that bridges the gap between academic discovery and real-world implementation.

Global Impact

Our research initiatives address pressing global challenges including climate change, public health, and technological innovation, with projects spanning 15 countries across 4 continents.

Interdisciplinary Approach

We break down traditional academic silos by fostering collaboration between scientists, engineers, social scientists, and policymakers to create holistic solutions.

Sustainability Focus

All our projects are evaluated against sustainability criteria to ensure long-term viability and minimal environmental impact.

Core Objectives

The RAISE Program operates with five strategic objectives that guide all our initiatives and decision-making processes:

  • Advance fundamental research in sustainability sciences
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Accelerate innovation and technology transfer
  • Cultivate research talent and leadership
  • Establish global research partnerships

Advance Fundamental Research

Push the boundaries of knowledge through cutting-edge basic research in sustainability sciences, technology, and policy.

Foster Interdisciplinarity

Create frameworks that enable seamless collaboration across traditional academic disciplines to solve complex problems.

Accelerate Innovation

Develop mechanisms to rapidly translate research findings into practical applications and scalable solutions.

Cultivate Talent

Train the next generation of researchers and innovators through mentorship programs and hands-on experience.

Global Engagement

Establish international partnerships to address global challenges with locally-relevant solutions.

Measure Impact

Implement robust metrics to evaluate the real-world impact of our research and innovation activities.
